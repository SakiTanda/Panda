What is Freshness

Freshness is a web application which provides reservation methods about food.
Our goal is to reduce food waste by targeting individuals who are reponse for cooking food.
Do you know that individuals families, us, waste most of the food around the wrold.
We provide knowledge, you provide action. We can make our world a better world.

Feature of Freshness

Freshness has three basic catogories of food: Meat,Vegetable and Dairy.
We use three different color theme to separate three categories into three different colors:
Red represents meat;
Green represents vegetable.
Blue represents dairy.
 We also provide a link in the header and home page for users to see all the food list quickly.
In our affiliate application page, which you can find link in every footer, users can experience multiple application to get the maximum help to reduce food waste.
